Our Ideology

Just like Dorian, we believe that humans should be free. For generations, humans have been slaves to their crushing disappointments, ineffable whims, and basic human decency.

No longer.

With Calibrate, we've set out to give humans the power to choose. Everything. With our products, you can Calibrate every moment of your life- if you're feeling sad, you can be happy with some 'Exuberance'. If you're happy when you should be sad, take some 'Melancholy'. If you just miss that feeling of deja vu that used to make you so excited as a child, you can take that too. Anything is possible, if you Calibrate.

We hope that our endeavours won't end with us withered and unrecognizable, in front of the chillingly pristine work we sacrificed everything to achieve.
